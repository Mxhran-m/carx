Professional Bike Service Guide for Optimal Performance

March 24, 2026 Car & Bike Care Garage 3 Comments

Your two-wheeler deserves the same care and attention as any four-wheeler. Regular bike servicing not only ensures optimal performance but also extends the life of your motorcycle. At Car & Bike Care Garage in Brookefield, Bengaluru, we specialize in servicing all bike brands including Royal Enfield, Honda, Bajaj, TVS, Yamaha, Suzuki, KTM, and more.

1. Regular Oil Changes

Engine oil is crucial for your bike's longevity. Change oil every 3,000-5,000 km or every 3 months, whichever comes first. Use manufacturer-recommended oil grade. Synthetic oils offer better protection and longer intervals between changes. Our service includes complete oil drain, filter replacement, and system check.

2. Chain Maintenance

The chain is the heart of your bike's power transmission. Clean and lubricate the chain every 500-800 km. Check chain tension regularly – too tight causes excessive wear, too loose risks slipping. Inspect sprockets for worn teeth. We recommend chain cleaning, lubrication, tension adjustment, and sprocket inspection during every service.

3. Brake System Service

Brakes are your most critical safety feature. Check brake pads for wear every 5,000 km. Replace brake shoes when lining thickness falls below 2mm. Ensure proper brake cable tension for cable-operated systems. For disc brakes, check rotor condition and brake fluid levels. Our comprehensive brake service includes pad/shoe replacement, fluid check, and system bleeding.

4. Air Filter Cleaning and Replacement

A clean air filter ensures proper air-fuel mixture for optimal combustion. Clean foam filters every 5,000 km and replace every 15,000 km. Paper filters should be replaced at recommended intervals. Clogged filters reduce performance and increase fuel consumption. We inspect, clean, or replace air filters as needed during every service.

5. Spark Plug Maintenance

Spark plugs ignite the air-fuel mixture in your engine. Check spark plugs every 10,000 km and replace every 20,000-30,000 km depending on type. Signs of worn plugs include difficulty starting, poor acceleration, and increased fuel consumption. We inspect electrode gap, clean carbon deposits, and replace worn plugs.

6. Clutch and Gear System

Smooth gear shifts indicate a healthy clutch system. Watch for signs of clutch slip or drag. Adjust clutch free play according to manufacturer specifications. Check gear lever and shift mechanism for smooth operation. Grinding gears often indicate low oil or worn synchros. Our technicians diagnose and repair clutch and transmission issues.

7. Suspension Service

Front forks and rear shock absorbers affect handling and comfort. Check fork oil for contamination every 20,000 km. Inspect fork seals for leaks. Adjust preload and rebound settings for your riding style. Rear suspension linkage should be lubricated regularly. We provide complete suspension service including oil change, seal replacement, and adjustment.

8. Electrical System Check

Modern bikes have complex electrical systems. Test battery voltage and charging system output regularly. Check all lights, indicators, and horn operation. Inspect wiring for damage or corrosion. Test kill switch and starter motor. We use advanced diagnostic tools to identify electrical issues in all bike brands.

9. Coolant System (Liquid-Cooled Bikes)

For bikes with liquid cooling, maintain proper coolant levels and replace every 2 years. Check radiator for damage and clean debris between fins. Inspect water pump for leaks. Coolant prevents overheating and corrosion. Our service includes coolant level check, system pressure test, and radiator cleaning.

10. Tyre Care

Tyres are your only contact with the road. Check tyre pressure weekly and adjust for load. Inspect tread depth – minimum 1mm for safe riding. Look for cuts, bulges, or uneven wear patterns. Replace tyres every 3-4 years regardless of tread wear. We offer tyre pressure check, wheel balancing, and tyre replacement services.
