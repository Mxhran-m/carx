Essential Car Maintenance Tips for Better Performance

March 25, 2026 Car & Bike Care Garage 5 Comments

Regular car maintenance is essential for ensuring your vehicle's longevity, safety, and optimal performance. At Car & Bike Care Garage in Brookefield, Bengaluru, we understand the importance of proper car care. Here are our expert tips to help you maintain your car and avoid costly repairs down the road.

1. Regular Oil Changes

Engine oil is the lifeblood of your vehicle. It lubricates, cools, and cleans engine components. We recommend changing your oil every 5,000-7,000 km or every 6 months, whichever comes first. Fresh oil ensures smooth engine operation and better fuel efficiency. Our expert technicians at Car & Bike Care Garage use high-quality synthetic and mineral oils suitable for all car brands.

2. Brake System Inspection

Your brakes are critical for safety. Have them inspected every 20,000 km or annually. Watch for signs like squeaking, grinding, or a soft brake pedal. Regular brake pad replacement and rotor resurfacing ensure reliable stopping power. Our brake service includes complete inspection, pad replacement, and fluid checks to keep you safe on Bangalore's busy roads.

3. Tire Maintenance

Check tire pressure monthly and before long trips. Under-inflated tires increase fuel consumption and wear unevenly. Rotate tires every 10,000 km for even wear. Inspect tread depth – replace tires when tread falls below 1.6mm. Proper wheel alignment prevents premature wear and improves handling.

4. Air Conditioning Service

Bangalore's climate makes a working AC essential. Have your AC serviced annually to ensure optimal cooling. This includes gas refilling, compressor check, and condenser cleaning. We use eco-friendly refrigerants and provide comprehensive AC diagnostics for all car models.

5. Engine Diagnostics

Modern cars have complex engine management systems. Regular diagnostic checks can identify issues before they become major problems. Our advanced diagnostic equipment reads error codes from your car's ECU, helping us pinpoint issues accurately and provide cost-effective solutions.

6. Fluid Levels

Check these essential fluids regularly:

  • Coolant: Prevents engine overheating – check monthly
  • Brake Fluid: Essential for braking – replace every 2 years
  • Power Steering Fluid: Ensures smooth steering
  • Transmission Fluid: For smooth gear shifts
  • Windshield Washer Fluid: Essential for visibility

7. Battery Care

Car batteries typically last 3-5 years. Clean terminals regularly and check for corrosion. Test battery charge before long journeys. If your car struggles to start or lights dim, have the battery tested. We offer battery testing, replacement, and jump-start services.

8. Suspension Check

A well-maintained suspension ensures comfort and safety. Signs of wear include bumpy rides, pulling to one side, or uneven tire wear. We provide complete suspension services including shock absorber replacement, strut mount repair, and wheel alignment.
